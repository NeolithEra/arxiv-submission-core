Entry-point for the submission worker application.

The heart of the worker is a Celery application that listens for tasks on a Redis queue. Tasks are event callbacks defined in rules. Tasks are identified by name, and get registered by the tasks.is_async() decorator. Importantly, this means that registration of tasks is a side-effect of importing rules.
